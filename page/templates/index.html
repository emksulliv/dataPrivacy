What does this all mean?

Accessing Data: So how does this work?

Ya know when a personality quiz asks for access to your facebook profile? What does that really even mean for you and your data when you accept that access?

Or here's another one... Know how some apps give you the option to either create an account with that specific application or (and usually the faster, easier entry point option) you can instead connect your Facebook account.

While easier and definitely faster, there is a catch. Whenever you link your Facebook account to a third party app, you're giving that app permission to access information from some parts of your personal profile.

Developers can request to see your relationship status, education history, religious or political beliefs, and many other data topics. But only if you allow them access to do so. This is known as "Third Party Apps" that log into your social media profiles and collect information.


Where does my data go?

Wherever the developer wants! No seriously. The use of data collected by third party apps is rarely disclosed.

While most social media will have a strict "Platform Privacy" that outlines what developers of third party apps can and can't do with regards to user data, these will vary from company to company


How can I control the information third party apps have access to?

There are a few options here...

  • Read!

    • when providing access to a third-part app. You can usually review the info you are providing. If you are not comfortable with giving this data - then do not connect your social media accounts.

  • Secure Your Info!

    • You can change the permissions you give a third-party app at any time. Social Media services will usually have a section in their settings that will list the third party apps currently having access to your data. You can remove apps from these lists to take away access to your data completely or take away certain data access rights (just access to photos, just friends list, etc)

  • Be confident in your info!

    • You can also choose to pick through your own profile and remove data you are not comfortable with third parties getting access to.
